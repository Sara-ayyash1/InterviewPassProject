About Interview Pass

Bridging the Gap Between
Talent & Opportunity

Interview Pass is a smart hiring platform designed to streamline the recruitment process for HR professionals while giving job seekers a structured and fair application experience.

Our Mission

To reduce time-to-hire for companies while giving candidates a meaningful, transparent, and organized application experience that truly reflects their potential.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform where every hiring decision is made with clarity, every candidate is evaluated fairly, and every opportunity is matched with the right talent.

What We Offer

Everything you need for a smarter hiring process

Smart Job Posting

HR managers can post detailed job listings with custom interview questions tailored to each position.

Structured Applications

Job seekers answer role-specific interview questions when applying, giving HR a complete picture from day one.

Real-time Tracking

Candidates track their application status in real time — pending, accepted, or rejected — with full transparency.
